About

Purpose

The mission of this organization to advocate the use of, educate potential users of, and support current users of the Linux operating system.

Membership

Membership shall be open to any KSU student, faculty, or staff, or any person in the surrounding community who wishes to take part in the Linux movement.

Membership shall uphold the the KSU non-discrimination policy.

Membership is open to all persons in the community. All members must be be subscribed to the organization mailing list.

The purpose and membership was taken from the constitution & bylaws.
